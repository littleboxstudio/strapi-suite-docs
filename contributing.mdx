## Donations
To stay completely free and open-source, we need your help. If you like our work, please consider donating to help us fund the project's future development.

We run our company independently, without outside funding, and rely on your help to keep the project going. This means we can concentrate on serving real users like you, instead of focusing on investors or profits. If you choose to use our self-hosted version, you have the opportunity to support our work. [Talk to us](/community-support) to find out how we can help you with your project.
